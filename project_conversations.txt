### Walkthrough / Outcome
We've overhauled the gameplay to be more competitive and polished.

#### 1. Smarter Input
- **Prefix Isolation**: You no longer need to type "Carrer de", "Avinguda", etc. The game automatically displays the prefix (e.g., "Carrer de...") and you just type the name (e.g., "Balmes").

#### 2. 3-Attempt Challenge & Navigation
- **Submit / Next Button**: A large, easy-to-use button helps you submit answers and move to the next question.
- **Attempts**: You have **3 attempts** per street, clearly shown with a "TRIES" indicator.
    - If you guess wrong, the input shakes and you lose an attempt.
    - After 3 strikes, the round is marked as failed and you can click "Next Street".

#### 3. Speed Tracking
- The game now covertly tracks how fast you answer each question.

#### 4. Visual Polish
- **Target Street**: Now a **Navy Blue** line.
- **Hints**: Now a bright neon blue to match the "target" style, as requested.

#### 5. Share Your Score
- At the end of the game, a new **Summary Screen** shows your Score and **Average Speed**.
- Click **Share** to copy your results or invoke the system share sheet.
